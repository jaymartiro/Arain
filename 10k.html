10K Roses

Located in Cordova, Cebu, 10000 Roses Cafe and More is famous for its thousands of artificial roses that light up at night. These LED-powered roses provide a pretty background to your travel photos.

The Korean-owned caf√© was inspired by the Dongdaemun Design Plaza in Seoul. It opened in 2017 and has since been serving guests coffee, pasta, pastries, salad, sandwiches, and more. Visitors can choose to enjoy the drinks and food indoors or in the alfresco area. There is also a viewing deck on the second floor, which provides an unobstructed view of the 10,000 roses, as well as the sea and the Cebu City skyline.
